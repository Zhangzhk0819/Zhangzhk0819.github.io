Research Interest

My recent research concentrates on differential privacy, machine learning privacy and their interactions. Specifically, I am interested in appling differential privacy (as well as local differential privacy) to enhence privacy for different data analysis tasks. Besides, I am also interested in evaluating the privacy leakage risks of different machine learning models (e.g., membership inference, property inference, etc), and denfending these potential threats using differential privacy.
